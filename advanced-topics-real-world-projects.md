*   **Define a Clear and Manageable Project Scope - Avoid Scope Creep Pitfalls - Project Scope Sanity = Project Success! üéØ: Scope creep (the insidious tendency for projects to expand uncontrollably beyond their initial boundaries) is a notorious project killer, especially in data science, where it's easy to get lost in endless data exploration and feature engineering rabbit holes.
